    • Zombie Snake Monsters Simulation!!!

    In this game there 3 types of characters
    Humans (White Circles)
    The Lazy Hero (Blue Circles)
    Zombie Snake Monsters (Red Circles)

    Humans (White Circles)
    -Run from zombie snake monsters
    -Collides with another human to have a chance of make a new human (a new baby that grows into a full human instantly)

    The Lazy Hero (Blue Circles)
    -Will do what ever he or she wants
    -If comes in contact with a Human there is a chance of turning human into another hero
    -If comes in contact with a zombie snake monster
    --- the monster might die
    --- the monster might turn back into a human

    Zombie Snake Monsters (Red Circles)
    -Will attack humans
    -If come in contact with human
    ---the human might turn into zombie snake monster
    ---human might die

    Also this whole entire thing started when the government did a secret test and accidentally polluted the air.
    Every once in a while the zombie snake monster plague will turn a random human into a zombie snake monster
    because the human accidentally breathed in the polluted air.
